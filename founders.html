FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free? What’s the catch?

Yes, it’s genuinely free for 6 months. No credit card required. In exchange, we ask for product feedback, usage data (aggregated and anonymized), and the opportunity to share your story as a case study (with your permission). After 6 months, you can continue on a paid plan or export your data.

What happens after the 6-month free period?

You’ll have the option to continue on a paid plan (with a founder discount), or you can export your audit logs and compliance data. There is no lock-in and no surprise charges.

I’m building a consumer AI product. Can I still apply?

Yes, as long as AI is your core product. However, teams building for enterprise, regulated, or high-trust use cases will be prioritized. Consumer AI products that handle personal data, health data, or financial data are also a great fit.

Can I use my own LLM API keys for deep compliance analysis?

Yes. Our BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) feature lets you connect your own OpenAI, Anthropic, or other LLM API keys for deeper compliance analysis. The core rule engine runs without any LLM dependency.

What jurisdictions and regulations do you cover?

LogionOS covers 4,004 compiled regulations across 6 jurisdictions: US (HIPAA, SOX, state privacy laws), EU (GDPR, EU AI Act), Japan (APPI), UK, Singapore, and Hong Kong. Pre-built policy packs are available for HIPAA, GDPR, EU AI Act, SOX, APPI, and more.

How long does it take to integrate?

Most teams integrate LogionOS in under 30 minutes. It’s a single API call with our Python or JS/TS SDK. No infrastructure changes needed — just add a compliance check before your AI outputs reach users.

Is my data safe? What data does LogionOS collect?

Your data stays yours. LogionOS processes compliance checks in real-time and stores audit logs. We only use aggregated, anonymized data to improve our models. Detailed data rights and privacy terms are provided in the program agreement. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

I’m not based in the US. Can I still apply?

Absolutely. This is a global program. We welcome teams from any country. Our platform supports regulations across 6 jurisdictions, and our team speaks English, Chinese, and Japanese.
